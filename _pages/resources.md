## Applications

### Notes

* [Evernote](http://www.evernote.com) - One of the handiest note/knowledge apps around
* [Quiver](http://happenapps.com/#quiver) - Notebook app similar to Evernote but with features for power users and developers

### Mind Mapping

* [Mindmeister](https://www.mindmeister.com/?r=47052) - Full featured online mind mapping
* [Mindmup](https://www.mindmup.com) - Zero friction, free, online mind mapping
 
### Tasks / Work Tracking

* [MeisterTask](https://www.meistertask.com) - Simple and beautiful Kanban-style task mangement that (optionally) integrates easily with Mindmeister and already most of the features of Trello
* [LeanKit](http://www.leankit.com) - A visual project/product delivery tool. One of the best SaaS Kanban tools I have used.
* [Trello](http://www.trello.com)
